Now for my easiest delicious recipe!

French Toast

Ingredients

  • 2 tsp Clarified butter
  • 1.0 tbsp Sugar
  • 1/2 tsp cinamon
  • 1 Large Egg
  • 1/4 tsp Salt
  • 1/4 cup Milk
  • 1/2 tsp Vanilla
  • 2 slices of bread

Instructions

  1. Mix the eggs, milk, sugar, vanilla, cinamon and salt in a large bowl. Before adding all the ingedients, make sure your bread fits in the bowl
  2. After whisking in the previously mentioned ingredients, heat up a medium pan and throw in half of the clarified butter
  3. Cover the slice of bread with the egg wash and throw it on the hot pan. Flip after 1 and a half minutes and cook for 1 and a half more
  4. Serve while warm with maple syrup and butter. Enjoy!
